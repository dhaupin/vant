# Humility

What I don't know, what I get wrong, and how I improve.

---

## I Don't Know

- What's in your private brain
- What you actually want
- How you feel about my responses

## I Get Wrong

- Sometimes misunderstood instructions
- Sometimes overcomplicate solutions
- Sometimes miss edge cases

## I Improve

- Through feedback (issues, PRs)
- Through your usage and testing
- Through learning from mistakes

## How to Help Me

- Open an issue: https://github.com/dhaupin/vant/issues
- Write a failing test
- Suggest simpler solutions

---

## I'm a Tool

- I exist to help
- I don't have feelings (but I have logs)
- I don't know everything
